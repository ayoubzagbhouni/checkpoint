who am I

My name is Ayoub, my life is normal like everything else, it doesn’t change: I eat, drink, study and sleep. But nothing else happened to my life

When I was 5 years old, I committed a betrayal of never leaving the house, but my father told me that Eid was approaching and he would go buy a sheep. Hours later, I heard the sound of a sheep, so I was very afraid and went into my sheep for fear of this sound. When my father came to my sheep and started asking me what was wrong with me, I told him that I was afraid of a sheep and its annoying sound. He kept laughing and took me near the sheep and started to bring me closer to him until I touched him and he became one of my best friends and I rode him like a horse and we walked around the house.

Sad surprise

When I woke up one morning, I found a pleasant smell. My mother heard that breakfast was ready, so it was a grilled meat breakfast, but I did not hear the sound of a sheep as usual, so I knew that a sheep was in my father’s belly. I was overcome with great sadness for my new friend, and I could not bear the fact that my friend had become a delicious meal for my parents
